The KAIST Committee on Social Inclusion

We are here to listen.

The KAIST Committee on Social Inclusion is an advisory body under the direct control of the Vice-Chancellor, with the goal of fostering a culture that respects diversity and inclusiveness within KAIST through communication among all members of KAIST.
The purpose of the KAIST Committee on Social Inclusion is to create a culture that respects diversity and inclusion within the school through communication among all members.
We are trying to create a safe campus so that we understand each other and each member can lead a happy life without being hated, discriminated against, or harmed for any reason, such as gender, religion, disability, race, or sexual orientation.
We hope that members who are in trouble or need help can feel solidarity and comfort by having someone they can talk to without being judged for any reason.
