KAIST Social Inclusion Committee

We are here to listen.

KAIST Social Inclusion Committee is an advisory body under the direct control of the Vice-Chancellor, established in 2017 to create a culture that embraces diversity on campus.
We aim for a campus where everyone can live happily without hate and discrimination based on gender, religion, race, physical disability, sexual orientation, etc.
We are always open to discuss ways to make it happen together and to help members in need.
